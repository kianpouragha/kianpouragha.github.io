About Us

Kian Pouragha

Founder and Metal Artist

Kian Pouragha is the founder and visionary behind Artifex Metalworks. With a deep-rooted passion for metal artistry, he combines creativity and craftsmanship to produce unique, luxury metal pieces. Driven by his love for design and attention to detail, Kian aims to create metal art that not only captivates but also stands the test of time.

Adrian Wei

Head Engineer

Adrian Wei is the engineering force behind Artifex Metalworks. As Head Engineer, Adrian brings precision and technical expertise to every piece. With a passion for innovation and a commitment to high-quality engineering, he ensures that every design is crafted with both form and function in mind.
